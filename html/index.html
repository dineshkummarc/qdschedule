Quick and Dirty Scheduler

This scheduler was created in one evening, and probably has bugs, but it works. Enter times in the form 8a - 5p for 8:00 AM to 5:00PM. Eventually maybe I will enhance the string parsing to handle minutes (decimals do work, so you can enter 8.25a - 9p for a 12.75 hour long day). Please note that the entire app runs client side, so I neither can nor want to save your data. The downside of this is that refreshing your data before exporting can be bad.
